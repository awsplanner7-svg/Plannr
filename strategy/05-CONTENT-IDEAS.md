# Plannr — Content Ideas (TikTok / IG / Shorts)

20+ post concepts ready to film. All faceless. All filmable in 15-30 min each. Tagged by format and effort.

**Strategy reminder:**
- The goal is comment engagement (people tagging friends) — not likes
- Every post should make someone go "this is my [friend name]"
- Plannr the app has a personality: dry, slightly tired, dramatic about group chat chaos
- Polished isn't the goal. Specific is the goal.

---

## Format Tags
- Screen recording (group chat / app)
- Mini scene (objects, B-roll)
- Static slides / text-only
- Voiceover suggested
- Pure meme format

## Post 1 — "The Group Chat That Never Plans Anything"

**Hook:** Fake group chat called **"emma's bachelorette"** blowing up.

**Content:** 15-sec sped-up footage of 47 unread messages where nothing gets decided. Includes "i'm down for whatever" three times. Ends with a hard cut to a clean Plannr board with city decided.

**Caption:** "tag the friend who started this chat 4 months ago"

**Why it works:** Universal pain. Tagging behavior built in.

## Post 2 — "Rank These Group Trip Friends"

**Hook:** "rank these group trip friends from worst to absolute menace"

**Content:** 5 static slides:
1. The Idea Veto (shoots down every suggestion)
2. The Late Payer (Venmo request still pending from 2 months ago)
3. The Last-Minute Itinerary Adder ("can we squeeze in a 7am hike")
4. The Ghost (agreed to come, hasn't responded in weeks)
5. The "I'm Down For Whatever" (never decides, complains after)

**Caption:** "ranked 1. ghost 2. veto 3. whatever 4. late payer 5. itinerary adder. fight me"

**Why it works:** Comment war guaranteed. Top engagement format on TikTok.

## Post 3 — "POV: You're the Type-A Friend"

**Hook:** Black screen text: "POV: you're the type-a friend in every group trip"

**Content:** First-person screen recording with text overlays. Series of moments:
- Everyone agrees the trip is happening
- You start a Notes doc
- You share it
- No one opens it
- You ask "thoughts?" three times
- Silence
- You open Plannr
- Their suggestions roll in

**Caption:** "if you don't have a type-a friend on the trip…you're the type-a friend"

## Post 4 — "Plannr's POV"

**Hook:** Text in first person from the app: "today i watched another group chat plan a trip"

**Content:** Sassy/tired voiceover (text-on-screen if no voice). Plannr describes watching a group chat spiral. Dryly notes the absurdity. Ends with "they don't know about me yet."

**Caption:** "i'm tired"

**Why it works:** Personifying the product like Duolingo. Builds character over time. Repeatable format.

## Post 5 — "The Notes Doc Funeral"

**Hook:** Black screen, sad piano music. Text: "in memory of every shared notes doc that died this year."

**Content:** 20-sec tribute reel of "dead" Notes/Google Docs:
- "NYC TRIP MARCH" — last edited 4 months ago
- "summer trip ideas??" — 2 edits, both yours
- "Camping?? TBD" — empty body, 7 collaborators

Ends: "they deserved better. your next trip deserves better." Cut to Plannr.

**Caption:** "rip"

**Why it works:** Highly memeable format. Reusable for other "in memory of" angles.

## Bonus: Posting Cadence Plan

**Week 1 (account warmup):**
- Day 1: Post 13 (comment bait — fastest to make, no UI dependency)
- Day 3: Post 2 (the friend audit ranking)
- Day 5: Post 5 (notes doc funeral)
- Day 7: Post 6 (things only the planner knows)

**Week 2 (build momentum):**
- Add Posts 1, 4, 8, 11 — start showing app UI

**Week 3+:**
- Lean into whatever's working in your analytics
- Reuse winning formats
- Start the @joinplannr "Plannr POV" voice as recurring series

**Posting rules:**
- Post 3-5x per week minimum to be visible to the algorithm
- Reply to every comment in the first 2 hours
- Save successful posts and replicate the format
- Don't delete underperforming posts (it doesn't help)
- Track what works in a simple spreadsheet (date, post, views, comments, follows gained)
