## 1. What This Policy Covers

This policy covers the Plannr mobile app and the backend service it talks to. It does not cover external websites you might tap through to (like Airbnb, OpenTable, or Google Maps) — those are governed by their own privacy policies.

## 2. Information Plannr Collects

### Information you provide directly

- **Account information.** When you sign up, you give us your name, email address, and password (stored hashed, never in plaintext).
- **Profile information.** Anything you choose to add to your profile, like a display name or avatar.
- **Content you create.** Boards, suggestions, checklist items, itinerary entries, votes, and any text, links, or images you upload.
- **Invite codes.** If you join a board via a code shared with you, we record your membership in that board.

### Information collected automatically

- **Usage data.** Basic information about how you use the app — which screens you visit, taps and actions — to help us identify bugs and improve the experience. We do not sell this data.
- **Device information.** Device model, OS version, app version. Used for compatibility and debugging.
- **Crash logs.** If the app crashes, technical details are sent to help us fix the bug.

### What we do NOT collect

- **Location.** Plannr does not track your location. If you tap a link to a place, the destination app (Google Maps, etc.) may use location — but Plannr itself doesn't.
- **Contacts.** Plannr never reads your phone's contacts.
